Physics Chapters

Classical Mechanics

Study of motion, forces, energy, and the fundamental laws that govern the physical world.

Thermodynamics

Analysis of heat, temperature, energy, and their relationships in physical systems.

Electromagnetism

Study of electrical and magnetic phenomena and their unified nature.

Waves and Oscillations

Understanding periodic motion and wave phenomena in various mediums.

Optics

Study of light behavior, including reflection, refraction, and optical phenomena.

Quantum Mechanics

Theory describing behavior of matter and energy at the atomic and subatomic scale.

Statistical Mechanics

Application of statistical methods to the behavior of large systems of particles.

Relativity

Einstein's theories of space, time, and gravity at cosmic scales.

Nuclear Physics

Study of atomic nuclei, nuclear reactions, and nuclear forces.

Particle Physics

Investigation of fundamental particles and their interactions.

Astrophysics and Cosmology

Study of celestial objects, universe structure, and cosmic evolution.
